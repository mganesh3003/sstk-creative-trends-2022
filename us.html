United States

Always the country to be bold, creative trends in the US for the coming year are waaay out there—flashy fashion, girl power, and fiery fantasy take center stage in 2022.

01.Boho-Chic Aesthetics

Retro style has never been more now. In the US, searches for the bohemian look are on the rise. Some search terms straight-up shout it—bohemian woman, boho picnic, and boho illustration, accented by phrases like retro prints, 70s floral, and psychedelic art. Consider giving your upcoming projects some throwback flair.

02.Action!

Female spies stealing top secret documents. Lady pirates outrunning and out-gunning a fleet of warships. A woman crashing through a window to escape from a bar (yes, woman and escape and bars is a trending search). More and more, creatives and showrunners are women who depict themselves the way we’ve always presented men—fearless and ready for action.
