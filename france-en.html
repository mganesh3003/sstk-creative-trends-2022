France

It can be scary to venture into unknown territory—or it can be a thrilling new adventure. In France, the big trends to watch come from both angles, with some creators diving into darkness, while others are embracing the adventurous spirit of exploration. Whether you’re looking to go far from home, or deep into the unknown, our data shows these trends will dominate the creative scene in 2022.

01.Mysterious Realms

Feeling spooky? French designers are embracing the darkness, with a frightful desire for imagery on the scary side. Searches for horror-themed content likewitchcraft, ghosts, anddevilish monstersare on the rise, withdark, mystique, andgrimoire rounding out a terrifying new trend.

02.Far-Off Flavors

France is known for its rich culinary tradition—but some creatives are looking beyond their borders and exploring cuisine from around the world. Searches for mochi, maki, sushi and nori are up, along with chili con carne and risotto, showing that French foodies are drawing inspiration from around the globe.
