Brazil

Our treasure trove of search data points us to an X marking the spot for next year’s big creative trends—and in Brazil it’s all about adventure—at sea, on land, and even at the dinner table. From the swashbuckling romance of pirate culture to jungles, giraffes, and mountain peaks, creatives are roaming inward and outward for inspiration.

01.Pirates on the Prowl

It’s no surprise, considering Brazil’s vast coastline, that the sea is always on trend. What is surprising is what our data says Brazilians are seeing on the horizon—both pirates and pirate ship are currently trending, along with bandana pattern, portholes and goblets. Perhaps it’s because design work that includes setting sail on the high seas in search of fortune and adventure is a welcome departure from our stay-at-home realities.

02.Flora and Fauna

Brazil is well-known for its stunningly diverse ecosystem, and we’re seeing a major trend as creatives explore the natural world. Searches for safari animals, forests, jungles, giraffes, and orchids are up. Would you include a T-rex in that set? Your call. But dinosaur vectors are far from extinct.

03.Eat, Drink and Be Creative

Of course Brazilian barbecue is trending upward, it’s an iconic flavor and perennial favorite. But in general, designers seem hungrier than ever—also on the rise are charcuterie spreads, baked goods, and fresh from the vine fruits and vegetables. Exquisite taste is always on trend.
